About SELFUNDR

Founded in 2018, SELFUNDR is committed to revolutionizing banking by providing cutting-edge financial tools to underserved communities. Our mission is to empower individuals with easy access to credit, investment opportunities, and comprehensive financial management. With a focus on technology, we connect entrepreneurs and investors through an integrated platform, featuring advanced credit scoring and a state-of-the-art trading room.
